About Realme 10

The Realme 10 has officially launched globally to deliver a balance of performance, design, and affordability.

Features

  • Display: 6.4-inch Super AMOLED, 1080 x 2400 pixels 409ppi
  • RAM: 4GB, 6GB, or 8GB + 8GB
  • Storage: 64GB, 128GB, or 256GB (expandable via microSD)
  • Camera: 50MP main camera, 2MP depth sensor
  • Battery: 5000mAh with 33W fast charging
  • Operating System: Realme UI 3.0 based on Android 12
  • CPU: Octa Core CPU

Design & Build

The Realme 10 has a simple, modern design with a plastic back and frame. It comes in two colors: Rush Black and Clash White. It's lightweight and stylish, making it practical for everyday use.

Performance

Powered by the MediaTek Helio G99 chipset with Mali-G57 MC2 GPU, the Realme 10 is designed for smooth multitasking and gaming performance with a 90Hz refresh rate. The phone also boasts a long battery life thanks to its 5000mAh battery.

Price

The Realme 10 is an excellent pick for customers on a tight budget who want a solid combination of features and performance because of its affordable cost.
