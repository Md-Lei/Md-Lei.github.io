About Realme 10

The Realme 10 combines sleek design, powerful performance, and budget-friendly features for everyday users.

Key Features

Display

6.4-inch Super AMOLED, 1080 x 2400 pixels, 409ppi

RAM & Storage

RAM: 4GB, 6GB, or 8GB + 8GB | Storage: 64GB, 128GB, 256GB (expandable)

Camera

50MP main camera + 2MP depth sensor for crisp photos

Battery

5000mAh battery with 33W fast charging for all-day use

Design & Build

Sleek and modern with a plastic back and frame, the Realme 10 comes in Rush Black and Clash White. Lightweight and stylish, perfect for everyday handling.

Performance

MediaTek Helio G99 chipset + Mali-G57 MC2 GPU ensures smooth multitasking and gaming. Combined with a 90Hz refresh rate and 5000mAh battery, the Realme 10 offers excellent efficiency.

Price & Value

Affordable yet powerful, the Realme 10 offers a premium experience without breaking the budget.
