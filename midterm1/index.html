THE QUEEN'S GAMBIT

The Queen's Gambit is an acclaimed Netflix limited series that premiered on October 23, 2020. Based on Walter Tevis's 1983 novel, it follows a young chess prodigy named Elizabeth Harmon as she rises from an orphanage to the world stage, portrayed by Anya Taylor-Joy. The story takes place in the 1950s and 1960s, and the series highlights Beth Harmon's rapid success in the male-dominated chess world while struggling with emotional problems, addiction, and the pressures of fame.

Renowned for its detailed storytelling, compelling performances, and authentic depiction of chess, The Queen's Gambit won 11 Primetime Emmy Awards, including Outstanding Limited Series, and earned Anya Taylor-Joy a Golden Globe for Best Actress. The show sparked a global resurgence in interest in chess, captivating both enthusiasts and newcomers with its rich narrative and visual intensity.
